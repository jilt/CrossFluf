## Challenge Tracks

### Track 1: Institutional Permissioned DeFi Vaults
- Compliant, permissioned vault infrastructure for institutional capital
- KYC/AML requirements for institutional participants
- Regulatory-aligned access controls

### Track 2: Cross-Border Stablecoin Treasury
- Cross-border payment corridors using stablecoins
- Treasury management across jurisdictions
- Compliance and settlement requirements

### Track 3: Programmable Stablecoin Payments
- Programmable payment infrastructure
- Automation, conditional logic, smart-contract-driven flows
- Institutional payment use cases

### Track 4: RWA-Backed Stablecoin & Commodity Vaults
- Real World Asset tokenization + stablecoin infrastructure
- Commodity-backed vault systems
- Bridging physical assets with on-chain mechanisms

## What Would Win

### Most Competitive Approach for Our Project

We're building a **Token-2022 KYC stablecoin + institutional permissioned vault** which maps to:
- **Track 1** (Institutional Permissioned DeFi Vaults) - PRIMARY
- **Track 4** (RWA-Backed Stablecoin & Commodity Vaults) - SECONDARY

**Why our project is competitive:**
1. Token-2022 KYC stablecoin = exactly what "institutional stablecoin infrastructure" means
2. Transfer hooks for on-chain KYC = regulatory alignment judges want
3. Vault with rebalancing (Ranger Finance style) = institutional-grade DeFi
4. Connection to FLUF flash loans = unique innovation differentiator
5. Using same architecture as PayPal PYUSD = proven, credible approach

**Key differentiator vs competitors:** Most teams will build simple DApps. We're building *infrastructure* - a full compliance-first stablecoin with vault mechanics. This is exactly what AMINA Bank would pilot.

### To Maximize Chances

1. Show working testnet demo (not just code)
2. Emphasize FINMA/MiCA compliance alignment in pitch
3. Highlight PYUSD-equivalent architecture (proven by PayPal)
4. Demo the KYC flow end-to-end: account creation -> KYC verification -> transfer hook enforcement
5. Show vault rebalancing with real protocol integrations
6. 3-min video: focus on the "why" for institutions, then show the product
